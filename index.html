Arcadia eSports is a vibrant community founded in 2023, dedicated to fostering a passion for technology and gaming among enthusiasts from all walks of life. Since our inception, we have been committed to providing a platform where individuals can come together to explore, learn, and celebrate the ever-evolving world of eSports.


EVENTS AND TOURNAMENTS


Throughout the year, Arcadia eSports hosts a variety of engaging events aimed at both beginners and seasoned gamers alike. From our renowned Tech Day, where participants delve into the latest advancements in gaming technology, to our Orientation Program, designed to welcome newcomers and integrate them into our vibrant community, we strive to offer something for everyone.

GLITCH 2024


At the heart of our club's mission is our main focused event, Techfest (GLITCH 2024). This annual extravaganza serves as a culmination of our efforts, bringing together gaming enthusiasts, industry professionals, and innovators for a celebration of all things eSports. GLITCH 2024 promises to be our most exhilarating event yet, featuring thrilling competitions, insightful panels, and opportunities for networking and collaboration.
